# 4. Business Workflow

---

# 4.1 Overview

Random Frames OS is designed around the actual workflow of the business.

Every module is connected so that information entered once automatically flows throughout the system.

The workflow begins when a potential client is identified and ends when that client becomes a repeat customer.

# 4.3 Stage 1 – Lead Generation

Lead sources include:

- Instagram
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Referral
- Website
- Phone Call
- Walk-in
- Networking Event

Information captured:

- Business Name
- Contact Person
- Industry
- Instagram
- Email
- Phone
- Location
- Lead Source
- Initial Notes

---

# 4.4 Stage 2 – Outreach

Communication methods:

- Instagram DM
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Phone Call

The system records:

- Date
- Time
- Platform
- Message
- Response
- Next Follow-up Date

---

# 4.5 Stage 3 – Opportunity

When a lead shows interest, it becomes an Opportunity.

Information captured:

- Interested Service
- Estimated Budget
- Expected Shoot Date
- Meeting Date
- Client Requirements

---

# 4.6 Stage 4 – Quotation

The quotation module generates professional quotations using:

- Pricing Manager
- Package Templates
- Custom Line Items
- Discounts
- Terms & Conditions

Possible outcomes:

- Accepted
- Pending
- Rejected

---

# 4.7 Stage 5 – Client Onboarding

After quotation approval:

- Client profile created
- Service Agreement uploaded
- Documents stored
- Advance payment recorded
- Project created automatically

---

# 4.8 Stage 6 – Project Management

Every project contains:

- Project Details
- Services
- Timeline
- Assigned Tasks
- Shoot Date
- Deliverables
- Client Documents
- Files
- Notes

---

# 4.9 Stage 7 – Production

Production includes:

- Photography
- Videography
- Drone (Future)
- Audio (Future)

Outputs:

- Raw Images
- Raw Videos
- Behind-the-scenes Content

Files are uploaded directly to Google Drive.

# 4.10 Stage 8 – Post Production

Stages include:

- Backup
- Culling

# 4.11 Stage 9 – Client Review

---

# 4.15 Workflow Rules

Business rules:

- A Lead cannot become a Project without becoming a Client.
- Every Client must originate from a Lead.
- Every Project must belong to one Client.
- Every Invoice belongs to one Project.
- Every Payment belongs to one Invoice.
- Every File belongs to one Project.
- Every Reminder belongs to one business record.
- Every interaction is timestamped.

---

# 4.16 Automation Points

The system automatically:

- Creates Clients after quotation approval.
- Creates Projects after booking.
- Schedules reminders.
- Creates Google Calendar events.
- Creates Google Drive folders.
- Generates quotations.
- Generates invoices.
- Tracks overdue payments.
- Sends payment reminders.
- Tracks recurring clients.

---

# 4.17 Workflow Completion

The workflow ends only after:

- Payment received.
- Project archived.
- Client follow-up scheduled.
- Client added to recurring marketing.

This ensures every completed project has the opportunity to generate future business.
