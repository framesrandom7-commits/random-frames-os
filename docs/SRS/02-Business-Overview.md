# 2.2 About Random Frames

Random Frames is a creative visual production company specializing in commercial photography, videography, and content production for modern businesses.

The company focuses on creating high-quality visual content that strengthens brand identity, improves digital presence, and helps businesses communicate more effectively with their customers.

Rather than operating as a traditional photography studio, Random Frames positions itself as a visual content partner for growing brands by providing strategic photography, videography, and recurring content creation services.

| Invoices | Manual Documents |
