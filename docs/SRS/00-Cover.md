# What is Random Frames OS?

Random Frames OS is a custom Business Operating System built specifically for Random Frames, a commercial photography and videography business.

Unlike traditional CRM software, Random Frames OS manages the complete business lifecycle from the first client interaction to project completion, payment tracking, and long-term client relationship management.

The platform centralizes sales, communication, project management, finance, reporting, automation, and business analytics into one integrated system.
