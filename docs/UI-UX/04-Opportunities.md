# Opportunities Module

---

# Purpose

The Opportunities module manages qualified leads that have shown interest in Random Frames' services.

It serves as the bridge between a Lead and a confirmed Client by tracking meetings, quotations, negotiations, booking probability, and expected revenue.

---

# Objectives

- Track potential bookings.
- Monitor sales progress.
- Increase conversion rates.
- Forecast future revenue.
- Convert opportunities into confirmed clients.
