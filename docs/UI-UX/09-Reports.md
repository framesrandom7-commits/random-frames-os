# Reports & Analytics Module

---

# Purpose

The Reports & Analytics module provides actionable insights into the overall performance of Random Frames. It consolidates sales, projects, finance, marketing, and client metrics into visual dashboards to support informed business decisions.

---

# Objectives

- Monitor business performance.
- Measure sales effectiveness.
- Track financial growth.
- Analyze project efficiency.
- Improve client retention.
- Identify business trends.
